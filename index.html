ÓMÓS

Mostly Irish Traditional Music from Central-ish Indiana

Ómós [OO-mahs] is Indiana’s newest Celtic band, comprised of veteran musicians with long individual resumes. The group’s name means “homage” or “tribute” in the Irish language, and the members have a deep respect for the traditions of Celtic music, including jigs and reels, ballads and humorous songs. At the same time, though, the band seeks to add its own material and contemporary arrangements of older songs and tunes in order to offer a new musical direction while still staying true to tradition.
